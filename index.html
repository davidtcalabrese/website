De Stilj

De Stilj is Dutch for "The Style". Proponents of De Stilj advocated pure abstraction and universality by a reduction to the essentials of form and color. In software, a universal design aethetic speaks to a wide audience.

Design

This is kind of a neat design, don't you think? Straight lines, basic shapes, simple colors. A strong sense of symmetry, balance and cohesion. Very simple, yet elegant, like well-designed software.
